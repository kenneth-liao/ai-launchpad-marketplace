## [1.0.0] - 2025-12-06

### Added

- **`business-competitor-analysis` skill** -- Comprehensive competitor analysis with target customer profile, market positioning, pricing, features, SWOT, and competitive matrix
- **`competitive-analysis-framework.md` reference** -- Detailed guide with market gaps methodology, competitive moats, threat analysis, and strategic recommendations
- **Web Researcher agent** -- General-purpose research agent for web search, data gathering, and insight synthesis
- Target customer profile (firmographics, psychographics, behavioral patterns, market sizing)
- Top 5 competitor identification and research workflow
- Market gaps and opportunities analysis
- Source link attribution in reports
- Save direction for output files
